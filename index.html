Welcome

  • Uga Tech is a homegrown technology company based in Lira City, dedicated to bringing modern digital solutions to businesses, schools, and local government. From affordable IT support to custom software and digital skills training, we help our community work smarter and grow faster.

  • We understand the unique challenges of northern Uganda limited internet access, lack of local tech talent, and high costs of outsourced services. That’s why we build practical, low bandwidth solutions and train young people right here in Lira to become tomorrow’s developers, technicians, and digital entrepreneurs.

  • Whether you need a website, a school management system, or reliable computer repairs, Uga Tech is your neighbor and partner. We don’t just sell technology – we invest in Lira’s long‑term digital transformation.
