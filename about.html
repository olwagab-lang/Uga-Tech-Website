About Uga Tech

Deep dive into Lira’s homegrown digital transformation hub

Mission & Vision

Mission: To democratize technology for small businesses, schools, and communities in northern Uganda by offering affordable, low‑bandwidth digital tools and practical training.

Vision: A digitally empowered Lira City where every market vendor, boda rider, and student can access and use technology to improve their daily lives.

Unlike large outsourced IT firms, Uga Tech is physically rooted in Lira. Our team speaks Luo and English, understands local payment habits (MTN Mobile Money, Airtel), and designs solutions that work even with unstable internet.

What We Do : Real Impact in Lira

Uga Tech offers three core services tailored to Lira’s needs:

  • Digital literacy intro 2‑day workshops at Lira Vocational Institute for small business owners.
  • Affordable web design & WhatsApp catalogues Starting at UGX 150,000 per website.
  • Hardware repairs & IT support – On‑site repairs for schools and offices in Lira City.

In just the first week of operation, Uga Tech onboarded 3 pilot clients: a phone repair shop near Boroboro Market, a tailoring cooperative, and a primary school in Adyel division.

Why Lira? : Under-served & Ready

Lira City, with a population of over 100,000, is a commercial hub for northern Uganda. Yet most tech services are based in Kampala, leaving local businesses with slow, expensive support. Uga Tech fills this gap by providing on‑ground, same‑day assistance and training locals to become self‑sufficient.

According to a 2025 Lira Chamber of Commerce survey, 78% of small businesses lack a basic website or digital catalogue. Uga Tech aims to reduce that to 40% by 2027.
